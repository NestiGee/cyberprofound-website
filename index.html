Our Approach

The principles that govern every decision

01

Disciplined Acquisition

Every acquisition is evaluated against defined financial and operational criteria — revenue, margin, client retention, and leadership continuity. We do not move for speed, and we do not overpay.

02

Long-Term Ownership

We deploy permanent capital. We do not manage to a fund cycle or an exit timeline. Every firm we acquire is held with the intention of owning indefinitely.

03

Shared Infrastructure

Acquired firms gain access to shared AI-driven detection and response infrastructure, centralized compliance and legal support, and capabilities they would otherwise have to build alone. How it works ›

04

Operational Excellence

Institutional governance structures and defined operating frameworks drive consistent performance across the portfolio. We bring board-level discipline to founder-owned cybersecurity firms.

05

Risk Governance

Each portfolio firm operates within a governance framework that holds it to defined security controls, regulatory requirements, and client commitments. Compliance is a condition of ownership, not a milestone.

06

National Scale

Strategic acquisitions fill geographic and capability gaps — building a firm with coast-to-coast presence across managed security, identity and access, and cloud security services.
