The Platform

One platform.
Built deliberately.

Cyber Profound is a U.S.-based cybersecurity consolidation platform. Our mandate is to acquire best-in-class managed security, identity, and cloud security firms and integrate them under centralized governance, shared operational infrastructure, and a unified national strategy.

01

Disciplined acquisition

We pursue a small number of high-quality firms, evaluated through a rigorous, repeatable diligence framework. We say no often — so that every yes strengthens the platform.

02

Long-term ownership

No forced exits. No countdown clock. We invest with a permanent-capital mindset — giving portfolio companies the stability to grow their capabilities and serve their customers for the long term.

03

Platform value creation

Centralized governance, shared operations, a shared AI security layer — capabilities no single mid-market firm could build alone become standard for all of them.

Why now

A $266B market,
fragmented and ready
to consolidate.

The U.S. cybersecurity services market is large, growing, and remarkably fragmented — thousands of high-quality regional firms, many led by founders approaching succession, most lacking the scale to invest in shared operations or national reach.

Traditional private equity answers this with 3–5 year flip cycles that disrupt teams and customers. We answer it with permanent capital and operational discipline. The consolidation wave is coming — we intend to build the platform that does it right.

Acquisition Criteria

Disciplined by design.

We are selective. We acquire profitable, well-run U.S. cybersecurity firms with durable customer relationships and leadership we want to keep. Specificity is our discipline.

Revenue $3M–$20M Annually. Sweet spot $5M–$15M ARR with demonstrated stability.
Profitability 15%+ EBITDA margin. We pursue healthy businesses — not turnarounds.
Recurring Revenue 70%+ Of total revenue. Durable, contractual customer relationships preferred.
Customer Base Low concentration High retention. No single client representing outsized dependency.
Leadership Builders Founders and operators who want to keep building — not exit and disappear.
Posture Certified SOC 2 Type II and/or ISO 27001. Clean transferable ownership structure.

Our Process
