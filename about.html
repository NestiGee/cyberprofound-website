About Cyber Profound

Building America's preeminent
cybersecurity platform.

Cyber Profound is a privately held U.S. cybersecurity consolidation platform focused on acquiring, unifying, and scaling best-in-class cybersecurity enterprises into a single, national-scale operation.

Our Mission

To consolidate the fragmented U.S. cybersecurity services market — managed security, endpoint protection, identity and access management, and cloud security — into one integrated, nationally scaled platform that delivers stronger protection, better outcomes, and lasting value for clients, employees, and stakeholders.

Our Vision

To become the preeminent U.S. cybersecurity services platform — a trusted national institution that sets the standard for operational excellence, governance, and client protection in an industry defined by fragmentation and eroded trust.

The Opportunity

A market defined by fragmentation

The U.S. cybersecurity services market represents a massive and growing opportunity. With global cybersecurity spending projected to exceed $500 billion by 2030 and cybercrime damages reaching an estimated $10.5 trillion annually, the demand for trusted, comprehensive security services has never been greater.

Yet the market remains deeply fragmented — thousands of regional providers operating independently, without the scale, governance, or resources to meet the evolving threat landscape. This fragmentation creates inconsistent service quality, duplicated costs, and gaps in protection that adversaries exploit every day.

Cyber Profound exists to solve this problem. Through disciplined acquisitions, we bring together high-quality cybersecurity firms under one platform with centralized governance, shared resources, and a unified national strategy — creating value for the companies we acquire, the clients they serve, and the industry as a whole.

Our Approach

Permanent capital. Disciplined governance.

Unlike traditional private equity, we don't acquire companies to flip them on 3-5 year timelines. Cyber Profound is built on a permanent capital model — we invest for the long term, giving portfolio companies the stability and investment horizon they need to truly grow.

01

Selective Acquisition

Every target is evaluated through rigorous diligence focused on financial resilience, leadership quality, customer loyalty, and strategic fit within our national platform.

02

Operational Integration

Acquired firms are integrated into a unified platform with centralized governance, standardized best practices, shared infrastructure, and a common national strategy.

03

Value Creation

We drive growth through cross-selling, operational efficiencies, stronger compliance postures, and the competitive advantages that come with national scale.

04

Long-Term Ownership

Our permanent capital structure allows us to prioritize sustainable growth over short-term exit strategies, building genuine enterprise value over time.

Who We Are

Led by operators, not financial engineers

Cyber Profound is led by a distinguished board of directors and senior operators whose collective experience spans more than four decades of U.S. cybersecurity innovation, enterprise technology leadership, national infrastructure protection, and mergers and acquisitions.

Our leadership team brings over 400 years of combined executive and cybersecurity expertise — including experience at Oracle, IBM, HSBC, and other global institutions — guiding our strategy, governance, and national roll-up execution.
