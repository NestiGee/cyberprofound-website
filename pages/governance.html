Governance Standards

Institutional discipline — from day one.

Board Oversight

Independent board governance

Board composition includes independent directors with deep domain expertise. Formal governance committees established at founding stage — not retrofitted after scale.

AI Governance

Responsible AI framework

A formal framework for every AI model deployed — across portfolio companies and internally. Model risk, data handling, auditability, and explainability are non-negotiable governance standards.

Compliance

Platform-wide compliance posture

Every portfolio company is held to the same risk and compliance framework: SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, NIST, and sector-specific regulatory alignment. Our own standards are part of what we sell.
