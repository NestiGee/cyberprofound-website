About Cyber Profound

Permanent Capital. Permanent Commitment.

Cyber Profound is a U.S.-based holding company that acquires, integrates, and scales managed security service providers. We operate under board-led governance, deploy permanent capital, and take a long-term view of every firm we own. We do not manage to an exit. We manage to endure.

The Firm

A Permanent-Capital Holding Company

Cyber Profound was founded on a single observation: the managed security service industry is essential, fragmented, and structurally underserved by capital. Thousands of firms protect hospitals, utilities, and government agencies — yet most operate without institutional support, succession plans, or the infrastructure required to compete in an AI-driven market.

We were established to address that. Our model is not private equity. We do not acquire to optimize and sell. We acquire firms that meet our criteria, integrate them into a shared operational and technology platform, and hold them indefinitely under institutional governance. The firms we acquire remain independent in brand and client relationship — they gain the capital, infrastructure, and leadership that most could not otherwise access.

How We Operate

Four Principles That Govern Every Decision

I

Permanent Capital

We do not manage to a fund cycle or an exit timeline. Every firm we acquire is owned with the intention of holding indefinitely.

II

Board-Led Governance

Every acquisition is governed by a board with direct cybersecurity, legal, and financial expertise. Governance is not a formality — it is how we protect the firms we own.

III

Operational Independence

Acquired firms retain their brand, leadership, and client relationships. We add infrastructure and institutional support — we do not absorb or rebrand.

IV

Disciplined Capital Deployment

We evaluate every acquisition against defined financial and operational criteria. We do not move quickly for the sake of speed, and we do not overpay.
