About

Building America's preeminent
cybersecurity platform.

Cyber Profound is a privately held U.S. cybersecurity consolidation platform — acquiring, unifying, and scaling best-in-class cybersecurity firms into a single, disciplined, national-scale operation.

Mission

To strengthen America's cybersecurity.

To strengthen America's cybersecurity by uniting proven firms into one disciplined, well-governed platform — so customers get fewer vendors and better protection, and the people who built these firms get a permanent home to keep building.

Vision

The preeminent permanent-capital platform.

To become the preeminent permanent-capital platform in U.S. cybersecurity services — the trusted acquirer founders seek out, and the national provider customers rely on. We are at the beginning of that journey, and we are building it deliberately.

The Opportunity

Why U.S. cybersecurity consolidation is accelerating.

01

Fragmented market

Thousands of regional providers operating independently — unable to deliver consistent, enterprise-grade protection at national scale. The top 10 cybersecurity services firms control less than 15% of the market.

02

Succession wave

A generation of founder-led cybersecurity businesses approaching succession, with no clear path to a buyer who will respect what they built and keep growing it — rather than cutting and flipping.

03

Demand consolidation

Enterprise buyers want fewer vendors, stronger assurance, and consistent SLAs. The market is demanding the kind of scale and integration only a disciplined platform can provide.

04

AI is redefining delivery

Threats now move at machine speed. A shared AI security layer — built at platform scale and deployed across every portfolio company — creates a capability moat no standalone mid-market firm can match.

Our Approach

How we acquire, integrate, and scale.

01

Disciplined acquisition

We pursue a small number of high-quality firms, evaluated through a rigorous, repeatable diligence framework. We say no often — so that every yes strengthens the platform. Revenue, retention, leadership, posture — all must clear our bar.

02

Long-term ownership

We invest with a permanent-capital mindset. No forced exits, no countdown clock — just sustainable growth and disciplined governance. We own companies because we want to build them, not because we need an exit.

03

Platform value creation

Each firm is integrated into a unified national platform: centralized governance, shared operations, a shared AI security layer, and a single national strategy. The capabilities no single mid-market firm could build alone become standard for all of them.

04

Operational excellence

We bring institutional discipline to entrepreneurial firms — structured processes, strong governance, centralized back office — that drive consistent performance without smothering what made each firm great.

05

Risk management

Every portfolio company is held to a comprehensive risk and compliance framework. In this industry, our own standards are part of the product we sell to clients.

06

National scale

We will build coast-to-coast coverage by acquiring firms that fill geographic and capability gaps — deliberately, one strong partner at a time. Not through acquisition for its own sake, but through selection for fit.

For Founders

Common questions.

What types of cybersecurity companies do you acquire?
We focus on established U.S.-based cybersecurity services firms — including managed security services, identity and access management, cloud security, and related professional services — with proven revenue, strong client retention, and quality leadership. Revenue $3M–$20M, EBITDA 15%+, recurring revenue 70%+.
Will my team and culture be preserved after acquisition?
Our model is built on retaining the talent, leadership, and culture that made your company successful. We are operators, not extractors. Founders who want to keep building continue to run their businesses — with institutional resources behind them rather than against them.
How long does your acquisition process take?
Timelines vary based on complexity, but our typical process — from initial conversation through due diligence to close — runs 60–120 days. Initial conversations are always confidential and carry no obligation.
How is Cyber Profound different from private equity?
Unlike traditional PE, we don't acquire to flip on 3–5 year timelines. Cyber Profound operates on a permanent capital model — we own companies for the long term and focus on building lasting enterprise value. There is no forced exit clock for the founders we partner with.
What happens to my brand and client relationships?
We respect the brand equity and trust you've built. Client relationships are preserved and strengthened — your clients gain access to expanded capabilities and resources. Sub-brand structures are designed to preserve market identity while enabling platform integration.
