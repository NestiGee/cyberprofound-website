Leadership Appointment March 2026

Gautam Banerjea Appointed Chairman of the Board

Cyber Profound is pleased to announce the appointment of Gautam Banerjea as Chairman of the Board of Directors. Mr. Banerjea brings extensive experience across private equity, institutional capital, and enterprise technology — credentials that directly align with Cyber Profound's mandate to build a permanent-capital cybersecurity platform of institutional scale.

His appointment reflects our conviction that governance is a competitive advantage, not a compliance exercise. A board led by individuals with direct experience deploying institutional capital into essential infrastructure markets is precisely the foundation a permanent-capital platform requires from its earliest days.

Under Mr. Banerjea's leadership, Cyber Profound's board will provide strategic oversight as the firm advances its acquisition pipeline, builds out national coverage, and establishes the governance infrastructure befitting a scaled cybersecurity platform.
