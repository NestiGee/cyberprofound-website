Leadership Appointment 10 March 2026

Mary Ann Davidson Appointed Chair of the Board at Cyber Profound

We are honored to welcome Mary Ann Davidson as Chair of the Board at Cyber Profound. Mary Ann is one of the most respected voices in cybersecurity, having spent nearly 40 years as Chief Security Officer at Oracle Corporation, where she led the company's global software security assurance program.

Her appointment reflects our commitment to building Cyber Profound with the highest standards of governance, expertise, and integrity as we grow a national cybersecurity services platform.

As a company built on the conviction that governance is a competitive advantage — not a compliance exercise — bringing Mary Ann's four decades of experience to the chair position is a statement of intent. She has shaped how enterprise software is built securely at one of the world's largest technology companies. That perspective is exactly what a cybersecurity consolidation platform should have at the board level from its earliest days.
